OUR STORY

Who we are

IVY Magazine Club was founded in Fall 2018 by two FIT students at SUNY Korea. The club brings together a diverse group of members across IGC to create dynamic editorials, interview students and professionals in the fashion industry, and capture campus life events.

Our goal is to put out high-quality, interesting, and fun content while giving our members an opportunity to explore their creative interests in fashion, photography, journalism, layout design, beauty, social media, and more!

What We Do

In addition to publishing a full-length magazine, IVY is active on SNS, posting on to our blog and Instagram account.

Since 2018, we have done various projects, including collaborating with well-known Korean fashion brands, writing Q&As with alumni in the industry, and producing special features on various topics.
